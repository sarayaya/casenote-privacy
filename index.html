中文版本

本应用（CaseNote）高度重视用户隐私与数据安全。

1. 数据存储方式

所有病例数据仅保存在用户设备本地。

开发者不会收集、上传、访问或共享用户录入的任何病例数据或个人信息。

2. 数据收集与使用

本应用不收集、不存储、不分析任何用户的个人身份信息。

所有信息仅用于用户本人在设备上的记录与查看。

3. 权限说明

本应用可能会请求以下系统权限：

  • 相机权限：用于拍摄图片并记录学习笔记
  • 相册权限：用于选择图片导入记录
  • 通知权限：用于提醒功能（如随访提醒）

所有权限均仅用于本地功能，不涉及数据上传。

4. 数据安全

所有数据均存储于设备本地，并受操作系统安全机制（如沙盒机制）保护，仅用户本人可访问。

5. 第三方服务

本应用不接入任何第三方数据分析、广告或数据收集服务。

6. 使用性质说明（重要声明）

本应用仅供医疗专业人士用于学习、记录与病例管理。

  • 不用于直接诊断或治疗患者
  • 不作为临床决策依据
  • 不替代医院信息系统（HIS / PACS 等）
本应用仅用于医生个人学习与病例管理，不提供任何医疗建议。

7. 隐私政策更新

如本隐私政策发生更新，将在本页面进行说明。

English Version

CaseNote respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data.

1. Data Storage

All case data is stored locally on your device.

The developer does not collect, upload, access, or share any user-entered case data or personal information.

2. Data Collection and Use

This app does not collect, store, or analyze any personal data.

All information is used solely for local recording and viewing by the user.

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: to capture images for notes
  • Photo Library: to select images
  • Notifications: to provide reminders, such as follow-ups

These permissions are used strictly for local functionality only.

4. Data Security

All data is stored locally and protected by the operating system’s security mechanisms, such as sandboxing.

Only the user can access their data.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

6. Intended Use

This app is intended for use by medical professionals for learning, documentation, and case management purposes only.

  • Not intended for direct patient diagnosis or treatment
  • Not a clinical decision-making tool
  • Not a replacement for hospital systems, such as HIS or PACS
This app is for personal learning and case management only and does not provide medical advice.

7. Updates to This Policy

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be reflected on this page.
