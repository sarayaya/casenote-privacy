# CaseNote 技术支持 / Technical Support

## 关于 CaseNote / About CaseNote

CaseNote 是一款病例学习笔记工具，帮助用户高效记录、归档和回顾病例学习资料。

CaseNote is a case study note-taking tool that helps users efficiently record, organize, and review case study materials.

## 功能概览 / Features

- 快捷病例录入与OCR识别 / Quick case entry with OCR recognition
- 典型影像资料归档 / Image archiving
- 复习计划与提醒 / Review plans and reminders
- 常用医学公式速查 / Medical formula calculator
- 学习统计分析 / Study statistics
- iCloud云端同步 / iCloud sync
- 中英双语界面 / Chinese & English interface

## 常见问题 / FAQ

**Q: 数据存储在哪里？/ Where is my data stored?**

A: 所有数据存储在您的设备本地和您自己的iCloud账户中，我们不会收集或访问您的任何数据。

All data is stored locally on your device and in your own iCloud account. We do not collect or access any of your data.

**Q: 如何备份数据？/ How to back up data?**

A: 打开设置页面，使用"备份与恢复"功能即可导出备份文件。开启iCloud同步后，数据也会自动同步到云端。

Go to Settings and use the "Backup & Restore" feature to export a backup file. With iCloud sync enabled, your data will also sync automatically.

**Q: 支持哪些设备？/ Which devices are supported?**

A: CaseNote 支持运行 iOS 17.0 及以上版本的 iPhone。

CaseNote supports iPhones running iOS 17.0 or later.
